How Does Tasky Work?

1

We send you
qualified leads

  • Customers come to Tasky looking to hire professionals for their projects
  • The customer fills out a form specifying the details of their project
  • You receive the customer request via email if it matches your preferences
2

Send a Quote

  • If you are qualified and interested in the job, you respond by sending a custom message and price estimate
  • Your contact information, reviews, and profile are automatically attached to your response
  • You only pay a small fee for this introduction using Tasky Credits when you send a quote
3

Get Hired

  • Typically, a few other professionals also respond to the customer. The customer compares the offers and chooses who to hire
  • The customer works out the details with you through Tasky, on the phone, or in person.
