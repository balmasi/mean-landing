What are Tasky credits and
what are they used for?

Credits are your virtual currency. When a customer request looks like a good match for your skills, you can use your Tasky credits to introduce yourself by submitting a quote.

How many credits would I need to
send a quote to a customer?

Depending on your industry,the number of credits required per introduction may vary. On average, each introduction costs only 2 credits. You can see how many credits are required when sending a quote.

How can I get more Tasky credits?

If you do not have any credits or have run out, you can always purchase more on our credits page. You can buy enought to send a single quote, or buy them in bulk at a discounted rate. Your credits will never expire!
